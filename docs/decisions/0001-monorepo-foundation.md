# 0001 Monorepo Foundation

## Decision

Use a monorepo with `apps/backend`, `apps/frontend`, `packages`, `docs`, `docker`, and `scripts`.

## Rationale

The requested product spans backend financial controls, frontend workflows, shared types, deployment assets, and documentation. A monorepo keeps early development coordinated and makes Docker-based onboarding simpler.

## Status

Accepted for Step 1.
